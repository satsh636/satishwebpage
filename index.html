About Me

I am a research scholar in the Department of Mathematics and Statistics at the Indian Institute of Technology Kanpur, pursuing my PhD under the supervision of Prof. Subhra Sankar Dhar. My research interest lies at the intersection of statistics and topological data analysis (TDA). In particular, the research I pursue is concerned with incorporating statistical techniques into the TDA framework as well as applying TDA methods in a complementary fashion to conventional statistical approaches.
